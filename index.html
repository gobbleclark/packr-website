FAQ

Frequently asked questions

We ship for dozens of brands. Does every brand get its own workspace?

Yes. Every brand you ship for gets its own channel and workspace — with its own branding, contacts, and SLA. Brands are unlimited on every plan, so adding your next account never changes the bill.

How is Packr different from Slack Connect or a shared inbox?

Conversations in Packr live next to the order data they're about, pulled live from your WMS. Messages thread by order, every request can become a task with an owner and an SLA, and edits write back to the warehouse — nothing gets lost in a forwarded email or a fourth Slack workspace.

How long does it take to get set up?

Connecting is instant — just your WMS login (email and password) and you're linked. From there we kick off a background job that pulls in your full order history, which can take around 7 hours to finish. You don't have to wait for it: you can start working in Packr the moment you connect, and your data fills in as it syncs. Start your 30-day free trial →

Which WMS do you integrate with?

Today we integrate with ShipHero. It's a deep, two-way sync — orders, holds, shipments, and errors all flow both directions. More WMS integrations are actively in development, so if you're on a different platform, talk to us about what's next on the roadmap.

How does the SLA work, and why is it more accurate than ShipHero's?

Packr measures your SLA from the order's allocation start date — the moment the order is actually ready to be worked — instead of the raw order date. That means holds don't count against you: if an order is paused waiting on a customer, a stock issue, or an address fix, the clock isn't punishing your team for time they couldn't act on. ShipHero's on-time numbers don't account for that, so the same warehouse looks better — and more honestly — measured in Packr.

Can Pax actually make changes to orders?

Yes. Pax can manage holds, change addresses, swap carriers, reprocess orders, and record shipping errors — all grounded in your WMS. Every action is permission-scoped and lands in a full audit trail, so your team always sees exactly what changed and why.

How many users and features do we get?

Everything is unlimited. Add your entire CX team and every one of your brands' teams — there are no per-seat fees, ever. Every feature is included too: Pax AI, WMS sync, threaded chat, tasks, holds, SLAs, surveys, and the full audit log. No tiers, no add-ons, no "contact sales to unlock." See the pricing →

Are we locked into a contract?

No contracts, ever. Sign up whenever you're ready and cancel any time — no annual commitment, no setup fees, no cancellation penalty. Start with a 30-day free trial and only keep going if Packr earns it.

What does it cost?

One usage-based plan: $8 per 1,000 orders, with unlimited brands, unlimited users, and every feature included. Start with a 30-day free trial — no contracts, no setup fees. Size it for your volume on the pricing calculator →
